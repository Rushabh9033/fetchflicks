How to Use FetchFlicks

A step-by-step guide to downloading videos from YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, and more.

  1. Find Your Video: Go to YouTube, Facebook, or Instagram and copy the link of the video you want to download.
  2. Paste the Link: On the FetchFlicks homepage, paste your video link into the input box.
  3. Choose Quality: Select your preferred resolution and format from the available options.
  4. Download: Click "Fetch Video" and follow the instructions to save the video to your device.

Safety & Legal Tips

  • Download videos only for personal use and with permission.
  • Do not share or distribute copyrighted content.
  • Respect the terms of service of each platform.
  • Check your device for enough storage space before downloading.
  • If you encounter issues, visit our Help or FAQ pages.
